Kay jawariyqa:
Tata Faustinu Camacho Sotop kawsayninpi qillqasqa. Anzaldo chirupi,
Kalallusta ayllumanta runakuna llakiyta kawsakuq kasqanku, chantapis kay
jawariyqa chaypi t'ukurispa qillqasqa kachkan.
Qillqaq
This story:
was written based on the life of Don Faustino Camacho Soto. The
people in the town of Kalallusta, in Anzaldo, lived a hard life, and so
this story was written with that in mind.
