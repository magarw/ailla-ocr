"Goodday, we are coming to talk with you,” mama Inkarna replied.
-"Yaykurimullaychik"- ñispataq mama Asteria, Fransiskap mamanqa wasin ukhuman,

"Come on in," said mama Asteria. Francisca's mother went into her house, where
they could talk more easily.
Yaykuytawanqa, wurumanta aqhata jurayk'achisqanku, mikhunatapis pampaman
muyullata churasqanku, ajinamantataq parlayta qallarisqanku:
They went in, unloaded the chicha from the donkey, and put the food on the floor
in a circle, and then they started to talk:
-"Kay qhari waway karnawal raymipi juchacharpakusqa, wawaykichikta wasiyman
pusamusqa"- ñispa tata Fransisku qallarisqa.
"My child, this man, has just sinned at Carnival, he brought your child to my
house," tata Francisco began.
