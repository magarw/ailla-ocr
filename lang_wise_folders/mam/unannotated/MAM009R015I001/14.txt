It is clear that the particular Mam categories within
the person/number system are a linguistic postulate of the
language and reflect a powerful and pervasive sub-structure
within the language. This and the identification of other
linguistic postulates within the language should contribute
considerably to other areas of study which must take into
consideration the language imposed categories which are
strongly tied to speaker perception.
