niwan chak k'enal, n (lum). Place name. Literally, Big Silty Shale. Chacquenal Grande,
a finca north of San Mateo where Santa Eulalia Kanjobal is spoken. See also yune chak
k'enal, place name, Finca Chaquenal, San Mateo, literally, Little Chaquenal,
Chaquenalito.
niwan k'op'an, n (lum). Place name: Montaña Nihuan Cobán, San Mateo. Literally, Big
Hole.
niwanp'ih, vin. To become larger or more numerous. Ex: tzonh niwanp'ih, We increase
in size or number.
tom niwan, exclam. You're welcome! Literally, It's no big thing!
niwan, n (ha'). River (any river). Ex: ha sniwanil, the course of the river, e.g., a branch we create
for irrigation. *sniwan.
