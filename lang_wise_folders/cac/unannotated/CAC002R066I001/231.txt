nuwup', n (lum). Place name: Nubup, San Mateo.
nux + Name, n phrase. A form of respectful address or reference to older females. Reciprocal
term of address is 'utzin. Cf. maj, older males; 'uch, younger females; naj, younger males. Ex:
nux malin, Old Mary. ha 'ix nux xuwan, that's Old Juana.
nwéwa 'esperánsah, n (lum). Place name: Finca La Nueva Esperanza, Nentón. Abandoned or
destroyed in the civil war (Manz 1988:89).
nwéwo konsepsyonh, n (lum). Place name: Finca Nueva Concepción, Barillas.
