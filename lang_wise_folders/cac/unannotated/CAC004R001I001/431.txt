yoktak ha yol sinhis, n (ha'). Place name: lower part of the Yolsinis, San Mateo.
Literally, Foot of the Yolsinis.
yoktil, n ('anh). Plant name. An edible mushroom. The foot is 6" in diameter, the top up to two
feet across. Black on top, white below, meaty like a pam p'u'uk. Grow in grasslands and among
pines.
yol, poss n. (Its) Center. A frequent element in place names. < y-'ol, cf. Kanjobal 'ul, inside,
interior ("adentro, interior." Diego de Diego Antonio et al., 1996, Diccionario del idioma
q'anjob'al. La Antigua Guatemala: Proyecto Lingüístico Francisco Marroquín.) Cf. yola', stream
(below).
yol 'ayin, n (ha'). Place name: lower part of the Río Chexjoj, San Mateo. Literally,
Crocodile Center (cf. 'ayin, a mythological animal; yola 'ayin, below).
yol 'icham, n (lum). Place name: Yolicham, a caserío of Jolomtenam, San Mateo.
Literally, Uncle/Old Man Center.
yol chonhap', n (lum). Place name: Yolchonab, San Mateo. Literally, Village Center.
yol k'e'en, n (lum). Place name: Cerro Yolquén, San Mateo. Literally, Rock Center.
yol k'uh, n (lum). Place name: ruins in the center of San Mateo, just south of the
waxaklajunh ruins. Literally, Sun Center (or perhaps earlier God Center).
yol k'ultak, n (lum). Place name. Yolcultac, Nentón. Abandoned or destroyed in the civil
war (Manz 1988:85). Literally, Brush Center.
yol makte mekel, n (ha'). Place name: part of the Río Pajelná, San Mateo. Literally,
Miguel's Corral Center.
yol nhajap', n (ha'). Place name: Laguna Yolnabaj [sic], Nentón. Literally, Lake Center.
yol sat k'e'en, n (lum). Place name: Olsatqueen, San Sebastián Coatán. Literally, Rock
Surface Center.
yol sinhis, n (lum). Place name: Yolsinis, San Mateo. Or, (ha') yoktak ha yol sinhis, a
stream running from Yolsinis to form a tributary of the Río Pacumal, San Mateo.
yol ta'anh, n (lum). Place name: a cantón of San Mateo Ixtatán, in the southeast section
of town. See Field Notes, August 31-September 6, 1964. One cemetery is located there,
and it straddles the road to Santa Eulalia. Literally, Ash (or Lime) Center.
