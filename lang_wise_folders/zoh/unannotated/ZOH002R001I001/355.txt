13.1.5. Progressive
The progressive in MIG Zoque is an auxiliary verb construction with the
auxiliary nəmmə (PROG). Neither the beginning nor the end of a situation is
visible from the progressive viewpoint, which "focus on the internal stages of
non-stative events" (Smith, 1991: 112). In narratives, the progressive is
generally used to present a background situation, one that is ongoing when the
event of importance occurs.
