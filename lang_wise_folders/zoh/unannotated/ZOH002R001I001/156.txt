6.3.2. Applicative hay
The function of the applicative suffix -hay is to create an additional core
argument, usually a recipient or beneficiary. Remember that NPs corresponding
to core arguments in MIG Zoque are not case-marked or otherwise
distinguished in any way. The use of the applicative suffix to add a new core
argument thus contrasts with the use of a postposition (+hənaŋ, ACCOM) or a
Spanish preposition (para, 'for') to indicate a non-core recipient or beneficiary.
