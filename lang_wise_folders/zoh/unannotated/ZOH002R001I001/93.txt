NOM1 is identified as a suffix to make this system work. It is possibly

that yields a pronominal form from the deictic root. There is no apparent
system or rule for choosing to use this suffix. The variant DEM form -gə
occasionally appears with DCT3: gagə, 'that', and even sometimes alone.
