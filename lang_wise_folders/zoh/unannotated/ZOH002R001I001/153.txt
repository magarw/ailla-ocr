The causative in MIG Zoque is for the most part quite straightforward.
One common usage involves the pairing of the causative with the antipassive.
It is easy to understand the function of this pairing in the case of an intransitive
verb that has been made transitive by adding the causative; if one then wishes
to suppress the object, the antipassive must be used.
