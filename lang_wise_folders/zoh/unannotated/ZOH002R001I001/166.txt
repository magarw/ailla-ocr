6.3.4. Antipassive ?oy
The antipassive suffix -?oy is added to a transitive verb to suppress the
object. The resulting meaning is generally 'Subj Verbs (something)'.
absolutive agreement markers are used with antipassive constructions. All of
the Zoquean languages have an antipassive affix, but none of the Mixean ones
do (Kaufman, 1996; Zavala, 2000b).
