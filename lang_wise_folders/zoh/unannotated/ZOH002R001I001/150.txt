6.3. Valency-changing suffixes
There is one valency-changing prefix: the causative yak-. There are four
suffixes that affect the argument structure of a verb: the passive -7ǝm, the
antipassive -?oy, the applicative -hay and the indefinite -Anəm.
6.3.1. Causative yak
The causative morpheme in this language is the prefix yak. Two
morphophonemic alternations occur with this prefix: gemination caused by
glottal stop deletion, and the obligatory merging of yak- and the prefix ?aŋ- to
produce yan-.
