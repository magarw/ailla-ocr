7.1.1.2. ?ok
This prefix usually, but not always, co-occurs with the suffix -?ǝy. ?ok-
combines with ?aŋ- to produce ?oŋ- (example vi), although this is a rare
occurrence. The most consistent contribution that ?ok- makes to the meaning
of the verb complex is BAJO, hence the gloss code. ?ok- and yuk-, ARR, are the
most consistently directional prefixes of the set. Finally, on a cultural note,
?ok- also indicates 'south', since one goes down in order to go south in San
Miguel Chimalapa, most clearly shown in example (v).
