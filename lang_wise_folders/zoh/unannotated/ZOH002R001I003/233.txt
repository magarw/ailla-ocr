stem; that is, for the verb stems to be linked in series and then affixed. The
causative always applies to the whole compound stem, as illustrated in (8.17 i),
as does the applicative suffix (ii). In example (iii), tuk-, 'to finish', is an
aspectual modifier of ?ek-, 'to harvest', which is a T3 verb that requires an
antipassive suffix in order to supress its patient. So, the antipassive pertains to
