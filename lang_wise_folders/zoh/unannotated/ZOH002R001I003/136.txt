clauses whose verbs have non-animate subjects in the texts, although the
lexicón contains many intransitive verb roots whose subjects must be inanimate
entities, such as puc-, 'rot'. Recipients are not undergoers in MIG Zoque, rather
they map to the third argument, discussed below. There are no examples of
sources as unmarked arguments.
